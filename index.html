My Projects

Singing Bee

Developed as a passion project by first-year technology students, Singing Bee showcases our creativity and skills while celebrating the power of music. This game was created solely for educational purposes, with the intent to entertain, and is free for anyone to play. We proudly emphasize that this is a non-profit initiative and a testament to what young minds can achieve.

LGU-CMS for Mindoro

The LGU-CMS (Local Government Unit Content Management System) is an innovative platform designed to streamline the digital operations of local government units in Mindoro. This system empowers municipalities to manage public information, services, and updates efficiently while fostering transparency and accessibility.

RedSource

RedSource is an innovative platform designed to revolutionize the management of blood donation and distribution. Created to bridge the gap between donors, blood banks, and hospitals, this system ensures the efficient management of critical resources while saving lives through streamlined operations.
